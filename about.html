A vision for the future of eSports.

eSports is lifestyle.

eSports is more than just a game. It’s a serious activity that involves research, strategy, money, and risks. However, the esports industry often struggles with issues such as fragmented experiences, accessibility barriers, and a lack of cohesive community engagement. Spectra: eSports is a project that treats esports for what it truly is – a high-quality software product.

Spectra: eSports was created by esports enthusiasts who saw an opportunity to meet new users’ expectations for digital experiences. We started with the goal of building the best free esports app out there, but we have an even loftier goal of transforming the esports industry.

If you are interested in our mission, feel free to get in touch. We are always happy to speak with designers, developers, esports players, bloggers, and investors.
