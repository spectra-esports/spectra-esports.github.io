A vision for the future of poker.

Poker is like investing.

It's a serious activity that involves research, strategy, money, and risk. So why does everything about the poker industry look and feel like a scam? Offsuit is a project that treats digital poker for what it really is - a software product.

Offsuit was created by two poker fans who saw an opportunity to match new users' expectations for digital experiences. We've started with the goal of building the best free poker app out there, but we have a loftier goal of changing the poker industry - one that has struggled with credibility issues, a steep learning curve, and hasn't been redesigned since the 90s.

If you're interested in the mission, feel free to get in touch. We're always happy to speak with designers, developers, poker players, and investors.
